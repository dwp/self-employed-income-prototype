Other expenses you can claim

Your expenses must be reasonable and relevant to your business.

Read the self-eployment guide your work coach gave you for a full list of expenses you can claim.

You might be able to claim if:

  • stock or raw materials
  • equipment and tools including purchase, hire or repair
  • travel costs like parking, tolls, congestion fees and public transport fares
    • advertising or marketing
  • office costs like stationary or phone bills
  • costs of business premises like heating, lighting, rent, water, charges, cleaning the premises and business rates
  • uniforms or protective clothing
  • financial costs suc as accounts, legal, insurance and bank charges
  • up to £41 for interest on business loans
  • staff costs such as wages or subcontractor costs

This is not a complete list.

You can also claim a flat rate if you use your home for business.

What you cannot claim

You cannot claim expenses for:

  • parking fines
  • travel between your regular place of work and home
  • business entertainment
    • shares
  • capital repayments on a loan
